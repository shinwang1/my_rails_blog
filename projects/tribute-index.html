Sir Anthony Hopkins

Actor, composer, painter

Relish everything that's inside of you, the imperfections, the darkness, the richness and light and everything. And that makes for a full life.

Here's a list of film awards he's won over his career:

  • 1972 British Academy TV Award for Best Actor - War and Peace
  • 1976 Emmy Award for Outstanding Lead Actor - The Lindbergh Kidnapping Case
  • 1981 Emmy Award for Outstanding Lead Actor - The Bunker
  • 1985 CableACE Award for Actor in a Movie - Mussolini and I
  • 1987 15th Moscow International Film Festival Award for Best Actor - 84 Charing Cross Road
  • 1991 Academy Award for Best Actor - The Silence of the Lambs
  • 1991 BAFTA Award for Best Actor in a Leading Role - The Silence of the Lambs
  • 1991 Boston Society of Film Critics Best Supporting Actor - The Silence of the Lambs
  • 1991 Chicago Film Critics Assoc Award for Best Actor - The Silence of the Lambs
  • 1991 Dallas Film Critics Assoc Award for Best Actor - The Silence of the Lambs
  • 1991 Kansas City Film Critics Award for Best Actor - The Silence of the Lambs
  • 1991 National Board of Review Best Supporting Actor - The Silence of the Lambs
  • 1991 NY Film Critics Circle Award for Best Actor - The Silence of the Lambs
  • 1991 Saturn Award for Best Actor - The Silence of the Lambs
  • 1993 BAFTA Award for Best Actor in a Leading Role - Shadowlands
  • 1993 LA Film Critics Assoc Award for Best Actor - Shadowlands
  • 1993 National Board of Review Award for Best Actor - Shadowlands
  • 1993 SE Film Critics Assoc Award for Best Actor - Shadowlands
  • 1997 Broadcast Film Critics Assoc Award for Best Supporting Actor - Amistad
  • 2003 Hollywood Film Festival Best Male Performer - The Human Stain
  • 2005 New Zealand Screen Award Best Leading Role - The World's Fastest Indian
  • 2006 Hollywood Film Festival Award for Ensemble fo the Year - Bobby
  • 2013 St. Louis Gateway Film Critics Assoc Special Merit - Hitchcock
